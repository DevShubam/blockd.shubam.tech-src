Type Definition:

Here is what the type column means:

Domains: This filter just has the raw domains, it will work with AdGuard, AdGuard Mobile, AdGuard Home, uBlock Origin and Pi-Hole.

Hosts: This filter has 0.0.0.0 infront of all domains, it will work with your hosts file, AdGuard, AdGuard Mobile, AdGuard Home, uBlock Origin, and Pi-Hole

ABP-Simplified: This filter has || before the domains, and ^ after the domains. This means it blocks that domain, and all of it's subdomains too (Unlike the top two). For example, "||example.org^" will block example.org, anything.example.org, example.org/anything and anything.example.org/anything. This will work with AdGuard, AdGuard Home, AdGuard Mobile and uBlock Origin.

AdBlocker: This is an advanced and most effective syntax. This will only work with AdGuard and uBlock Origin
